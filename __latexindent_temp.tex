\subsection{Sample Calculation}
To calculate the index of refraction, we apply Snell's law:
\begin{align}
    n_1\sin{\theta_1}&=n_2\sin{\theta_2} \label{snells_law}\tag{Snell's Law}, \\
    n_2&=\frac{n_1\sin{\theta_1}}{
        \sin{\theta_2}
    }.
\end{align}
